## 🚀 Overview

AI Salon Management System is an all-in-one solution for beauty salons, spas, and grooming businesses.

The platform simplifies appointment scheduling, customer relationship management, employee tracking, inventory monitoring, billing, and business analytics through intelligent automation and AI-powered recommendations.
